Here are the answers to the top 4 questions about $Genie we got on Twitter & Telegram.

What are the use cases of $Genie coin apart from just being a meme token?

First of all, $Genie is not just a meme coin, it's a whole DeFi ecosystem. As soon as we gain enough holders & attention, we will launch our utility services one by one which includes Genie Play, Genie Pad, Genie Lotto, Genie Swap, Genie Wallet, Genie Digital, and more. To know more about the above-mentioned utility cases, please read $Genie's WhitePaper.

How $Genie give BUSD rewards?

This is the specialty of $Genie Coin. It's the algorithm of the $Genie Coin's smart contract which gives BUSD as long as you're holding $Genie in your wallet. Whenever someone buys or sells their $Genie, the contract collects 14% fees from every transaction. 8% of the transaction fees go to existing holders in the form of BUSD. The contract doesn't give rewards in $Genie itself because like other cryptocurrencies in the market $Genie's value might fluctuate a lot. Therefore, if you get rewards in $Genie itself, the value of your rewards can go very high also very down, but when you get rewards in BUSD which is a stable coin(equals to a dollar), the value of your earning will be fixed.
